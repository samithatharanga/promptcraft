**PromptCraft** is a lightweight, blazing-fast, and 100% free frontend micro-tool designed to help normal users generate highly effective, structured prompts for Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, without needing to learn complex prompt engineering.

🌐 **Live Demo:** [https://samithatharanga.github.io/promptcraft/](https://samithatharanga.github.io/promptcraft/)

---

## 🎯 Why PromptCraft? (The Value Proposition)
In the era of AI, the quality of your output strictly depends on the quality of your input. Most people struggle to get good results from AI because their prompts are too vague. 

PromptCraft solves this by breaking down the "Prompt Engineering" process into 4 simple, highly structured fields: **Role, Task, Context, and Format**. It automatically concatenates your inputs into a master prompt that guarantees professional, accurate, and high-quality AI responses.

### ✨ Key Features
- **⚡ Real-Time Preview:** Watch your master prompt generate live as you type.
- **🎨 Tone & Format Selectors:** Instantly define the exact output style you need (e.g., Markdown table, Professional Email, Bullet points).
- **🔒 100% Privacy-Focused:** No backend, no databases, and no tracking. Everything runs locally in your browser.
- **📱 Responsive & Modern UI:** Built with Tailwind CSS, featuring a beautiful dark mode and glassmorphism design.
- **📋 One-Click Copy:** Ready to paste directly into ChatGPT or Claude.

## 👥 Who is this for?
PromptCraft is designed for anyone who uses AI to get work done:
* **👨‍💻 Software Engineers & Developers:** Generate precise code logic, debugging prompts, or architectural planning questions.
* **📈 Marketers & Copywriters:** Create highly targeted ad copies, blog structures, and social media posts.
* **📊 Data Scientists & Analysts:** Ask AI to format data queries, Python scripts, or data visualizations.
